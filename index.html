About Us

Located in Shenzhen, Guangdong Province, China, WDF Machinery is an integrated industrial enterprise engaged in the research, production, processing, domestic sales and international import & export of mechanical products. We have mature supply chains, standardized production workshops and strict quality control systems.


Our core products cover industrial processing equipment, automated machinery, general-purpose mechanical devices, customized mechanical spare parts and precision hardware fittings. Our products are widely applied in industrial automation, mechanical processing, equipment assembly and other industries.


We take Shenzhen as the operation center to serve global customers. We accept bulk orders, customized non-standard parts, OEM & ODM cooperation. Reliable quality and professional after-sales service have earned long-term partnerships with clients from all over the world.

Business Scope

Machinery Equipment Sales

Complete industrial machines, processing equipment, automated production lines and general machinery for industrial usage.

Mechanical Spare Parts Production

Customized structural parts, transmission components, wearing parts and standard accessories for all kinds of machinery.

Precision Hardware Components

CNC machined hardware, non-standard metal fittings, precision hardware parts for mechanical assembly.

Global Import & Export

International trading, customs declaration service, long-term overseas wholesale and cross-border supply cooperation.
